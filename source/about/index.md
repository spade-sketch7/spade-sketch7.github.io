I'm Rakan. I work in IT, based in Saudi Arabia — cloud, endpoints, security, the kind of infrastructure that has to just work.

Outside of that, I race. I take a car to the limit, learn what breaks, and fix it better than before. I tinker — some fabrication, some Arduino when a problem calls for it. I lean on AI as a tool for building and thinking faster, not a shortcut around understanding.

Mostly, I'm someone who likes figuring things out — at work, in the garage, wherever a problem shows up.

This site is where I write it down: the fixes, the builds, the things I learn. If any of it helps someone else figure out their own version, that's the point.
